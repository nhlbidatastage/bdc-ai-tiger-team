Pasta with Tomatoes and Basil
  1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling water according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add the tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes, or until the tomatoes are softened.
  4. Add the basil and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add the pasta to the skillet and toss to coat.
  6. Serve with Parmesan cheese and enjoy!
This dish is best served immediately. If you have any leftovers, store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. You can also freeze the leftovers for up to 3 months.
